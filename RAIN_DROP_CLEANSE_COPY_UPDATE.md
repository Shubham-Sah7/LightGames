## 📝 Copy Guidelines (For Future Updates)

### Welcome Screen
- Keep subheading to 2 lines max
- Use simple, inviting language
- Focus on the action ("Wipe away...")
- No long explanations

### Completion Screen
- Use casual, friendly greeting
- Keep it short (1-2 lines max)
- Lowercase for warmth
- No formal language
- Let mascot do the talking

### General
- Honeydew mascot speaks to user
- Warm, not clinical
- Friend, not therapist
- Show, don't tell
- Less is more
